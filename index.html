Eligibility Engine

Am I eligible?

Answer 4 quick questions and we'll calculate your exact match for Spain's Digital Nomad Visa (2026 rules).

01

Remote income requirement

You must earn at least €2,849/month (200% SMI) from remote work. 80%+ from non-Spanish clients.

02

Non-EU/EEA nationals welcome

Open to citizens of any non-EU/EEA country. EU/EEA nationals have simpler pathways.

03

Education or experience

University degree OR 3+ years relevant professional experience required.
